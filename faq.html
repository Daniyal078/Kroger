How It Works

An eSIM is the modern version of the old-fashioned plastic SIM cards which have historically been used in mobile phones as a piece of hardware that sits inside your phone. An eSIM is software that allows you to download a Sim instantly to your phone, allowing you to connect to a local mobile network almost anywhere in the world. An eSIM is a more convenient, and environmentally friendly way for you to connect. Simply download the Sim Local app today, activate a bundle, and go.

Our eSIMs are completely free to install. Whenever you need connectivity, you can purchase a plan via the SMS we send you.

For full instructions on downloading an eSIM and purchasing a plan, please visit our installation page.

Your data plan will activate as soon as you arrive in your destination country or region. If you are already in your desired destination when you book a plan, it will activate immediately.

Yes. Our eSIMs work seamlessly alongside any current SIM card or eSIM already installed on your device. Simply download a United States plan, and when you are in United States, you will be able to browse the internet and use apps at local data prices, instead of paying for international roaming charges. 

Our eSIM plans are data-only – meaning that they do not support the use of voice calls or SMS messages. You will still be able to make phone calls and send SMS messages in United States, however they will be billed to your usual network provider. Any internet-based messaging or calling services such as WhatsApp, iMessage, Telegram, Zoom or FaceTime can be used with a BetterRoaming eSIM however.
